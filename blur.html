The Requirements

CPU: Intel i5-4460 or AMD Ryzen 3 1200

RAM: 8 GB, Video Card: NVidia GTX 970 OR AMD RX 470

Dedicated Video RAM: 4096 MB

Pixel Shader: 5.1, Vertex Shader: 5.1

More information

Blur is a game completely focused on making racing fun again. Everything the player does, both in their cars and with their cars, is a dramatic adrenaline rush. Up to 20 cars on a track will battle each other as they trade paint, attempt hair-raising jumps, take shortcuts and collect power-ups, including the ability to boost their speed or blast other cars out of the way with huge bursts of energy. Developed by Bizarre Creations, the creators of the Project Gotham Racing series, Blur is out now for PC, PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360. www.blurthegame.com
