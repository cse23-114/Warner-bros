The Monkey Man

Monkey Man" is a 2023 action thriller film directed by Dev Patel, who also stars in the lead role. The film marks Patel's directorial debut and follows the story of a character named Kid, who seeks vengeance against corrupt leaders responsible for his mother's death and ongoing oppression of the poor. The narrative draws inspiration from the Hindu deity Hanuman, embodying themes of strength and courage.

Produced by a team including Jordan Peele, the film has been noted for its ambitious blend of action and socio-political commentary, although some critics have pointed out that its narrative can feel overstuffed and reliant on flashbacks. Despite these critiques, Patel's direction and the film's unique action choreography have been praised, positioning "Monkey Man" as a promising start for Patel's future in filmmaking​ (Universal Pictures)​​ (Roger Ebert.)
