The Bel Air

"Bel-Air," a dramatic reimagining of the classic sitcom "The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air," premiered in 2022 and continues to stream on Peacock. The series, developed by Morgan Cooper, is set in modern-day America and offers a deeper, more nuanced look at Will Smith's journey from the streets of West Philadelphia to the gated mansions of Bel-Air.

Overview of the Series

Bel-Air" follows Will Smith, portrayed by Jabari Banks, whose life takes a turn after a confrontation in his hometown forces him to move in with his wealthy relatives in Bel-Air.
