The NUN II

"The Nun II," a continuation of the chilling Conjuring Universe, was released in cinemas on September 8, 2023. Directed by Michael Chaves, known for his work on "The Curse of La Llorona" and "The Conjuring: The Devil Made Me Do It," the film is a direct sequel to the 2018 movie "The Nun."

Produced by James Wan and Peter Safran, the movie maintains the atmospheric and terrifying essence that fans expect from the Conjuring franchise. The sequel continues to explore the dark, supernatural elements that made the first film a global hit​ (whattowatch.com)​​ (Movie Insider)​.

Set in 1956 France, the plot follows Sister Irene, played by Taissa Farmiga, as she confronts the demon nun Valak, reprised by Bonnie Aarons. The story kicks off with the murder of a priest and the spreading of a malevolent force. Other cast members include Jonas Bloquet as Frenchie, Storm Reid, Anna Popplewell, and Katelyn Rose Downey.
